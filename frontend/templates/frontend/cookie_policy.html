Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and enable certain features to function properly.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website.

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and settings to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.

Analytics Cookies

We use these cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website, helping us improve our services.

Marketing Cookies

These cookies track your browsing habits to enable us to show advertising which is more likely to be of interest to you.

3. How We Use Cookies

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve our website's performance
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. These third parties may include:

  • Analytics providers
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Payment processors

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

To manage cookies, you can:

  • Use your browser settings to control cookie preferences
  • Use our cookie consent tool to manage preferences
  • Delete cookies through your browser settings

6. Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, limiting cookies may impact your experience of our website.

To learn more about cookies and how to manage them, visit:

  • www.aboutcookies.org
  • www.allaboutcookies.org
