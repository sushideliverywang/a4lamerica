About A4L America LLC

Established in February 2020, we persevered through the pandemic to serve our community with dedication and care. Over the past years, we've proudly served more than 4,000 satisfied customers throughout the Atlanta area.

Who We Are

A4L America LLC, operating as Appliances 4 Less, is a trusted provider of quality home appliances at affordable prices. Since our founding in February 2020, we've grown from a small operation to a cornerstone of the Atlanta appliance market, serving homeowners, contractors, property managers, and businesses throughout the metropolitan area and beyond.

We specialize in offering scratch and dent, open-box, new, and gently used appliances - helping thousands of families save money while getting the quality appliances they need. Even during the challenges of the pandemic, we remained committed to serving our community, adapting our operations to ensure safe and reliable service.

Founded on the principle that everyone deserves access to quality appliances without breaking the bank, we've built our reputation on three core values: transparency, fair pricing, and excellent customer service. Every member of our growing team shares these values and works hard to ensure every customer leaves satisfied.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to provide high-quality appliances at prices that make sense for your budget. We believe that minor cosmetic imperfections shouldn't prevent you from getting the appliances you need. That's why we specialize in scratch and dent products that function perfectly and come with full warranties, but cost 30-70% less than retail.

We're not just selling appliances - we're helping families furnish their homes affordably, assisting contractors in completing projects on budget, and enabling property managers to maintain their properties cost-effectively. Every dollar you save with us is a dollar you can invest in what matters most to you.

What We Offer

Scratch & Dent Appliances
